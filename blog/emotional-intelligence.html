What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence has two facets to it. The first is the ability to understand, and become the master of oneâ€™s own emotions. This can be shown through patience, carefulness, and controlling of oneself and their ego. The second facet is the ability to understand others, and to be sensitive, empathetic, and measured when interacting with people.

How is EQ different to IQ?

IQ describes general intelligence across multiple areas, particularly with regards to technical skill. EQ (Emotional Intelligence) covers a more specific area of intelligence relating to people, and being able to understand and be measured with others, and thyself.

Why is EQ important?

EQ is very important. Having a strong EQ can make you happier in life, form stronger relationships, make you more employable, allow you to be more content and resilient when times are rough. EQ is a general life skill that people can strengthen and improve on for the better of them, and people around them.
