The Principles of Neuroplasticity

Neuroplasticity is the phenomenon where neurons in our brain change and reorganize as we learn and 'do' over time. Understanding the principles of neuroplasticity enables people to recognize that they fundamentally can change and learn new information on a biological level.

Engaging with Neuroplasticity for Your Benefit

Neuroplasticity also teaches us that repetitive practice strengthens the connections between neurons, thereby making you better/stronger at whatever you are learning. An individual might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity when learning new skills such as a musical instrument. By applying principles such as "Practice makes perfect," an individual might be motivated to continue practicing their new skill, resulting in improvement.

How to Increase Your Neuroplasticity

Practice, Practice, Practice! Neuroplasticity is the ability for neurons to rewire over time, and this is constantly happening but can be sped up by continuing the same skill as often as possible!

Why Is a Growth Mindset Relevant?

Having a strong growth mindset can speed up your learning process exponentially. It relates to increased motivation, increased focus, and improved memory related to learning.
